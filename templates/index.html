Purpose

Often times students go into a class without any knowledge about the workload of the quality of the teacher. This makes it difficult to effectively make decisions on which class to take. The purpose of this database is to allow students to be more prepared and informed on the classes they are planning on taking.
This website is completely anonymous. Emails are not accessible. The purpose of this is to allow students to voice their opinions without worry of being identified.

Security:

Security is a top priority. All emails are hashed, all passwords are hashed and salted. Even as the owner of the database, I am unable to view the users. Emails are only saved as a hash in order to prevent multiple reviews per class and verifying MST email addresses. Forms are encrypted with standard internet protocol POST method, and the server is secure with Apache.
